Definition of Market Value

Market Value is the highest price estimated in terms of money which a property will bring if exposed for sale on the open market allowing a
reasonable time to find a buyer who buys with the knowledge of all the uses to which it is adapted and for which it is capable of being used.
Frequently it is referred to as the price at which a willing seller would sell and a willing buyer would buy.
